How We Make It

From Garden to Glass

01

Harvest

We source only the crispest, freshest cucumbers from local family farms — picked at peak ripeness each morning.

02

Brine

Our secret brine — pure water, natural sea salt, fresh dill, and spices — is prepared fresh for every batch.

03

Ferment

Patience is our secret ingredient. Each batch ferments slowly for 7–14 days for maximum probiotic goodness.

04

Jar & Ship

Hand-packed into glass jars and shipped straight to your door within 48 hours of completion. Always fresh.
